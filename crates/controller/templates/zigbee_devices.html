To add Zigbee devices, plug in a Zigbee coordinator (e.g. a CC2652 or Sonoff Zigbee 3.0 USB dongle) and add a [zigbee] section to config.toml, then restart home-edge. 

[zigbee]
serial_port = "/dev/ttyUSB0"   # path to your coordinator's serial port
adapter    = "auto"             # auto-detect adapter type (znp, ezsp, …)
# permit_join_on_startup = false

The Zigbee coordinator connects over a local serial port and works independently of how your phone connects to home-edge (WiFi or BLE).
